Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Thank you for using AI Answer Generator. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a safe and secure experience when using our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Query Information

When you use AI Answer Generator, we collect the questions you submit to generate answers. This information is necessary to provide our service and improve its functionality.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • The pages you visit on our website
  • How long you spend on each page
  • Your browser type and version
  • Your device type
  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Referring websites

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To respond to your queries and generate accurate answers
  • To improve our AI model and its responses
  • To monitor usage of our service
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information by our staff
  • Anonymization of usage data

Data Retention

We retain your query information for a limited period to improve our service and AI model. After this period, the data is anonymized or deleted. Anonymous usage data may be retained for a longer period for statistical purposes.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics tools and content delivery networks to improve our service. These third parties may have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about how you use our service. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
